The Why

A monthly gathering,
in my backyard.

Second Saturday Cinema is what it sounds like — on the second Saturday of each month, we pick a movie, set up a screen in my backyard, and invite friends over to watch it together.

I'm doing this because I think the small, repeated get-togethers are what actually build community, and I've wanted a standing reason to have friends at my place more often. One night a month feels about right.

I'm hosting at my house on purpose — I want it to feel like home. A shared evening with people I know. It's genuinely low-key: my house might not be clean, the popcorn might burn.
